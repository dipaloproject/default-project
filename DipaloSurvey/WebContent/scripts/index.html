Dipalo Project Survey

About this survey

Dipalo is an interactive online tutoring tool. Dipalo provides interaction between students from different schools across South Africa, to help each other with Mathematics. It also provide relevant Mathematics online resources for learners to access for FREE. Available online resources include videos, textbooks, past question papers and it allows learners to post homework questions from which one of our well-trained tutors will assist you through a LIVE chat and we will also post the most popular homework questions along with their solutions, nd these can be discussed by other learners too.
